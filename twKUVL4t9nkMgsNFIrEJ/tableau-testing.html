Instructions

Filtering Data: Use the checkboxes on the right to filter the locations by grant type—either MHSP (Mental Health Services Professional) or SBMH (School-Based Mental Health).

Searching: Use the "Search Map" bar to find specific locations. You can type the name of the city or state you are interested in.

Viewing Details: Click on any of the colored dots on the map to view details about that location, such as the city, grant number, grant type, grantee name, and partnership type.

Understanding the Map: Different colored dots may represent different types of grants or data points. Check out the legend explaining these colors.
